What is VedPay?

VedPay is an upcoming startup and wanted to develop the all in one payment platform with the most secure biometric authentication possible. Our basic idea is to integrate all the debit cards and credit cards to a single platform and users can easily pay their amount to merchants through our most secure vein scanner in just a few seconds & users don't have to carry their card(s)/phone/physical cash at all.

Our duty is to provide trustworthy solutions and services that allow our customers to achieve long-term market stability.
